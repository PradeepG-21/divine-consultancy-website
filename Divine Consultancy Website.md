# About The Founder
[[P. Ganesh]], has more than 25 years of experience in Small Industries Development Bank of India, an apex financial institution engaged in promotion, finance and development of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.

He has comprehensive experience of managing credit operations like sanction, disbursement, monitoring and follow-up with respect to loans at different levels in SIDBI.  He has significant exposure in handling operations related to business growth for profit maximization, cost reduction and productivity.  He is a Team Leader with strong communication, analytical, problem solving and organizational skills.  He opted VRS as Assistant General Manager from SIDBI with around 10 years of service still left for superannuation.

He has also served as Consultant with Grant Thornton Bharat LLP and was placed as Manager in SIDBI-PMU-Tamil Nadu for over 19 months. With rich experience of 25 years in SIDBI and 19 months as Consultant (Manager SIDBI-PMU-TN) in Grant Thornton Bharat LLP, he has developed good contacts with various machinery suppliers, service providers, industrialists and various industries associations. 

With such rich experience in serving MSMEs, he has now ventured into consultancy to facilitate loans for deserving MSMEs.
